# Resume

Add your resume PDF to this folder.

**Filename:** `resume.pdf`

Place your PDF at:
```
public/resume.pdf
```

The Contact section links to it as **Resume** (opens in a new tab).

---

**Alternative names:** If you prefer a different filename (e.g. `Ian-Kim-Resume.pdf`), either:
1. Rename your file to `resume.pdf`, or  
2. Update the link in `src/components/Contact.jsx`:
   - Change `href="/resume.pdf"` to `href="/YourFilename.pdf"`
